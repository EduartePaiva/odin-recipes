Pudding

Description

Pudding is my favorite dessert, it has many different recipes but I like the traditional one.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 cup of cow's milk
  • 4 whole eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar (tea)
  • ⅓ cup of water (tea)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. In a pan, mix the sugar and water and bring to medium heat, stirring constantly, until it forms a golden syrup.
  3. Pour the syrup into a pudding mold and set aside.
  4. In a blender, mix the condensed milk, milk, and eggs until you get a homogeneous mixture.
  5. Pour the mixture into the mold with the syrup.
  6. Cover the mold with aluminum foil and bake in a water bath for about 1 hour and 30 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely before unmolding.
